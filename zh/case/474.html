🎨 中文提示词

一个以参考图像为主题的前卫当代艺术展览空间。
整个展厅（20.0 米 x 20.0 米 x 8.0 米）将建筑、灯光、地板、墙面和天花板融入到艺术作品的表达中。
展厅最深处矗立着一面宽 20 米、高 8 米的大型墙体，墙体中央以巨大的艺术形式展现着参考图像中的主题。图像清晰立体，向观者升起，成为整个空间的焦点。
一块由系统生成的展览标题牌将被安装在墙体中央下方。标题必须抽象、象征且富有诗意，并且必须是当代艺术作品。展览不设价格显示。
地板采用反射率为 0.35 至 0.40 的抛光花岗岩。源自参考图像的图案和光线层层叠叠地铺于作品表面，与整个空间产生共鸣，仿佛在回应参观者的脚步。触觉铺路砖颜色相近，浑然一体，但高度仅为5毫米，触感清晰。作品从入口沿直线延伸至大墙，并在作品前形成一个停顿点。观展结束后，参观者自然而然地被引导至右侧的开口（3米宽 x 3米高）。在紧急情况下，地面应急照明可确保1勒克斯的照度。
左右两侧的墙壁和天花板分别以不同的方式诠释了参考图像中某个元素的抽象版本，使作品本身成为一件艺术品。色彩、形态和光线的流动将整个空间统一为一次艺术体验。
参观人数限制为8至25人。所有参观者面向大墙，沿着直线移动，并在停顿点停下。没有人回头看向入口。仅一名工作人员驻守在大墙右侧入口附近。所有面部均经过模糊处理，以保证匿名性。
构图稳定，中心消失点始终位于大墙中心。垂直度在±0.5°以内。地板反射精准，人体自然。双手始终为五指，双眼对称，偏差在3%以内。织物保持平直，无任何变形。
禁止内容：与参考图像无关的元素、缺失或破损的触觉铺路、面向入口的访客、徽标或水印、过度拥挤、玩具般的光泽、2D平板投影、霓虹灯辉光、青橙色调、过饱和、透视崩溃、反射不匹配、解剖异常、多余的肢体、扭曲的面部、过多的轮廓、条纹、晕影。
DoD：整个场馆将是一个围绕参考图像主题的当代艺术作品，整个空间以最内部的结构为中心，形成统一的体验。触觉铺装与光线流动完美同步，形成清晰的停留点。观众将沉浸在空间本身之中，即使在重现过程中，SSIM 也将稳定在 0.95 或以上。

🎨 英文提示词

A cutting-edge contemporary art exhibition space centered around a reference image.
The entire gallery (20.0 meters x 20.0 meters x 8.0 meters) integrates architecture, lighting, flooring, walls, and ceiling into the artistic expression.
At the deepest part of the gallery stands a large wall measuring 20 meters wide and 8 meters high, featuring a monumental artistic display of the theme from the reference image at its center. The image is crisp and three-dimensional, rising towards viewers and becoming the focal point of the space.
A system-generated exhibition title plaque will be installed below the center of the wall. The title must be abstract, symbolic, poetic, and must qualify as a contemporary art piece. No price display will be shown in the exhibition.
The flooring is polished granite with a reflectivity of 0.35 to 0.40. Patterns and light derived from the reference image are layered across the surface, resonating with the entire space as if responding to visitors’ footsteps. Tactile paving tiles with similar color are seamlessly integrated, only 5 millimeters thick, with a clear tactile feel. The artwork extends in a straight line from the entrance to the large wall, creating a pause point in front of the piece. After viewing, visitors are naturally guided toward the right-side opening (3 meters wide x 3 meters high). In case of emergency, emergency floor lighting ensures an illumination of at least 1 lux.
The walls and ceiling on both sides interpret abstract versions of specific elements from the reference image in different ways, transforming the artwork itself into a piece of art. The flow of color, form, and light unifies the entire space into a cohesive artistic experience.
Visitor capacity is limited to 8–25 people. All visitors face the large wall, move along a straight line, and pause at designated stopping points. No one looks back toward the entrance. Only one staff member is stationed near the right entrance of the large wall. All faces are blurred to ensure anonymity.
The composition is stable, with the vanishing point always centered on the large wall. Verticality is within ±0.5°. Floor reflections are precise, and human figures appear natural. Hands always have five fingers; eyes are symmetrical with deviations within 3%. Fabrics are kept flat without deformation.
Prohibited content includes: elements unrelated to the reference image; missing or damaged tactile paving; visitors facing the entrance; logos or watermarks; overcrowding; toy-like glossiness; 2D flat projections; neon glow; cyan-orange tones; oversaturation; perspective collapse; mismatched reflections; anatomical anomalies; extraneous limbs; distorted faces; excessive outlines; stripes; vignetting.
Design Objective: The entire venue functions as a contemporary art piece themed around the reference image, with the innermost structure forming a unified experience. Tactile paving and light flow are perfectly synchronized, creating clear focal points. Visitors will be immersed within the space itself, and even during reproduction, SSIM will remain stable at 0.95 or above.
