🎨 Chinese Prompt

使用我的面部特征，保持面部、眼睛、鼻子、嘴唇、耳朵和发型与参考照片完全相同（100%一致）。创作一个9:16电影感竖幅人像，画面为同一女性在户外下雪时站立，构图从大腿中部到头部。她微微向镜头一侧倾斜（侧身），头向上倾斜约50°朝向天空，一只手握着透明伞，另一只手伸出，手掌向上，仿佛雪花在落下。雪花停留在她的睫毛、面颊和外套上，在她泛红的皮肤上略微融化。她的表情平静且充满惊奇，电影感十足，像一场冬季爱情电影的场景。

🎨 English Prompt

Use my facial features, keeping the face, eyes, nose, lips, ears, and hairstyle 100% the same as the reference photo. Create a 9:16 cinematic vertical portrait of the same woman standing outside in the snow, capturing the photo from mid-thigh to head. She is leaning slightly to the side toward the camera ( profile), head tilted up about 50° toward the sky, holding a clear umbrella in one hand and the other extended outward, palm facing up as if snow is falling. Snowflakes linger on her eyelashes, cheeks, and coat, slightly melting on her red skin. Her expression is calm and full of wonder calm and cinematic, like a scene from a winter romance movie.

Outfit: Black wool winter coat, with thick knitted white scarf wrapped once around his neck (not covering jaw), hair tucked Inside scarf, no hat.

Lighting: Soft diffused daylight with cinematic HDR tones and natural cold atmosphere.

Camera: Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.2 lens, shallow depth of field, ultra-realistic optics, detailed snow particles, natural bokeh.

Mood: Emotional, reflective and beautifully sad
