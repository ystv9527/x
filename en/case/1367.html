🎨 Chinese Prompt

创作一幅逼真的撕纸拼贴肖像，完全由多层彩色纸片构成。
面部特征、头发、服装和阴影均由不规则手撕的纸片构成，粗糙且纤维状的纸边清晰可见。
每一层纸片自然重叠，产生微妙的层次感和纸片之间柔和的阴影分隔。
使用柔和的土色系与去饱和色调，并能看见纸张纹理和厚度。
不使用颜料，不留笔触——仅保留纸张质感。
背景由较大块的撕纸有机排列构成，采用柔和的中性色调。
柔和漫射的影室灯光增强纸层和边缘阴影效果，但不产生光泽或反光。
手工制作的编辑插画风格。
超细致、富有质感、具备博物馆级混合媒介外观。
方形构图，高分辨率。

🎨 English Prompt

Create a realistic torn paper collage portrait of the subject, constructed entirely from layered pieces of colored paper. Facial features, hair, clothing, and shadows are formed using irregular hand-torn paper fragments with rough, fibrous edges clearly visible.
Each paper layer overlaps naturally, creating subtle depth and soft shadow separation between pieces.
Use muted, earthy tones and desaturated colors, with visible paper grain and thickness.
No paint, no brush strokes — only paper textures.
Background made of larger torn paper sheets arranged organically, with soft neutral tones.
Soft diffused studio lighting enhances the paper layers and edge shadows without gloss or shine.
Handcrafted, editorial illustration style.
Ultra-detailed, tactile, museum-quality mixed-media look.
Square format, high resolution.
