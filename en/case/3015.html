The manga style should be Japanese shoujo manga line art, black-and-white with high contrast, delicate strands of hair, subtle blush lines, clean panel borders, and a white background.

The overall image should feel like a real person taking a selfie in her own manga world, with a natural fusion of real photography and black-and-white manga. The lighting should be clean and soft, the skin realistic and delicate, the subject should appear three-dimensional and crisp, the phone should be visible at the edge of the frame, and the composition should feel premium, magazine-poster-like, with high-definition details and cinematic quality.
