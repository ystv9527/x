🎨 Chinese Prompt

一幅超高清晰度、摄影质感极强的街头壁画，画面呈现强烈的中国风韵味。

画中描绘着一位绝美的卡通风女子正面特写头像，她神态柔美而宁静。墙体顶部被一大片盛开的蔷薇花覆盖，茂密的绿叶与繁盛的花朵向外舒展，部分枝条从墙顶垂落而下，与女子的头发巧妙融合，使她的秀发宛如由层层叠叠的蔷薇花组成。这些繁密的花朵簇拥着女子的头部，形成了一顶瑰丽的花冠，视觉效果华美浪漫。

背景中蓝天澄澈，点缀着朵朵白云；地面为一条细节真实的沥青街道，上面散落着缤纷多彩的花瓣，行人悠然漫步其间。整体场景细节精致入微，光影明亮柔和，营造出犹如现实般的梦幻街景氛围

🎨 English Prompt

A street mural with ultra-high definition and an extremely strong photographic texture, the picture presenting a strong Chinese style charm.

The painting depicts a close-up portrait of an exquisitely beautiful doll-like woman facing frontally, her expression soft and serene. The top of the wall is covered by a large expanse of blooming rose flowers, with dense green leaves and abundant blossoms extending outward, some branches hanging down from the top of the wall and cleverly blending with the woman's hair, making her locks appear as if composed of layers upon layers of rose flowers. These dense clusters of flowers surround the woman's head, forming a magnificent floral crown with a visually ornate and romantic effect.

In the background, the blue sky is clear, dotted with puffs of white clouds; the ground is a realistically detailed asphalt street, scattered with colorful petals, where pedestrians stroll leisurely. The overall scene is intricately detailed, with bright and soft lighting and shadows, creating a dreamlike streetscape atmosphere that feels just like reality.
