🎨 Chinese Prompt

今日は妻にAntigravity 導入ガイド。横で色々見てた娘が「AIはアタリの絵でも違う角度出せる？」と描いた絵を持ってきた。
全然違う仕上がった状態になるか、全然だめかのどっちかかも…と伝えてNano Banana Proで試したらこれ。

確かに目は描かれてしまったけど、ツインテール、フード、袖の形にスカートのプリーツにポーズまで、元の絵にあった娘の意図がちゃんと拾われてて、すげーすげー騒いでた。零れたのは胸元の紐がネクタイと判断されたとこぐらい。

ここまでラフでも回せる事もそりゃ凄いけど、拙い線としか見ていなかった私の認識の浅さと、きちんと伝わる表現をしていた娘の方に驚いた。

形は違えど、AIがこういう気付き与えることは無数にあるはずで、親や教育者の不明超える効果はこれから凄まじく広がってくと実感した。

🎨 English Prompt

Today I gave my wife an Antigravity setup guide. My daughter, who’d been watching from the side, brought over a drawing she’d done and asked, “Can AI produce different angles even from a rough sketch?”

I told her it might either end up as something completely different when finished or be a total bust, and tried it with Nano Banana Pro—and this is what we got.

It did draw in the eyes, to be sure, but it properly picked up my daughter’s intent from the original drawing—the twin tails, the hood, the shape of the sleeves, the skirt pleats, even the pose—so we were making a huge fuss about how amazing it was. About the only miss was that the string at the chest was interpreted as a necktie.

It’s amazing that it can run even on something this rough, but what surprised me was my own shallow perception, having seen it as nothing but clumsy lines, and my daughter, who had produced an expression that communicated properly.

Even if the form is different, there must be countless times when AI gives you this kind of realization, and I felt that its effect in helping to surpass the blind spots of parents and educators will spread tremendously from here on.
