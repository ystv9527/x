🎨 Chinese Prompt

一张戏剧性的黑白工作室肖像照。主体背对镜头站立，上半身轻微转向侧面，头微微低下，脸部侧面轮廓部分可见。表情平静而内省，营造出一种安静、神秘的氛围。

灯光：一盏强烈的侧光，投下鲜明的对比。一束干净的水平光束横切过背景墙。画面大部分处于阴影之中，着重强调剪影和轮廓。
氛围：极简主义，情绪化，电影感，唤起优雅与孤独感。主体身穿深色夹克，与阴影融为一体，呈现出流畅利落的时尚杂志风格。
构图：使用全画幅相机拍摄，85mm镜头，f/1.8大光圈营造浅景深效果，ISO 200，快门速度1/125秒。中景构图（从腰部到头部），突出剪影、侧脸和姿态。
后期：高对比度的黑白效果，柔和的色调渐变，深邃的阴影，添加轻微的胶片颗粒以增加质感，最终呈现精致的艺术杂志风格。

🎨 English Prompt

A dramatic black-and-white studio portrait. The subject stands with their back to the camera, torso slightly turned to the side, head gently lowered, revealing the profile silhouette of the face. The expression is calm and introspective, creating a quiet, mysterious atmosphere.

Lighting: A strong side light produces vivid contrast. A clean, horizontal beam cuts across the background wall. Most of the frame is in shadow, emphasizing silhouette and contours.

Mood: Minimalist, emotional, cinematic, evoking elegance and loneliness. The subject wears a dark jacket that blends into the shadows, presenting a sleek, fashion magazine style.

Composition: Shot with a full-frame camera using an 85mm lens, aperture f/1.8 for a shallow depth of field, ISO 200, shutter speed 1/125 sec. Medium shot (from waist to head), highlighting silhouette, profile, and posture.

Post-processing: High-contrast black-and-white effect with soft tonal gradients, deep shadows, slight film grain added for texture, resulting in a refined art magazine aesthetic.
