🎨 English Prompt

Modern Oriental Decorative Thick-Paint Portrait Style｜Mythical Character Poster Series
One point to note is that the character type will affect how the person’s style is presented. You need to add a description in 【Character/Theme】.

【Character/Theme】 is only for visual reference. Preserve the core identifying traits and translate everything consistently into the Modern Oriental Decorative thick-paint portrait style, combining Galang’s manner of character abstraction and deformation, Lin Fengmian’s Oriental lyrical decorative sensibility, and the Dunhuang mineral-color aesthetics. A half-body mythological character portrait: a slender, elegant Oriental oval face, simplified and sparsely spaced features, clean facial negative space, long, upturned eyes, and an air that is ancient yet restrained.

The overall composition is governed by large color blocks. Complete block areas establish the major relationships among the hair, face, clothing, and background; bright areas, gray areas, and dark areas are unified clearly, with edges tightened and loosened appropriately. Use a soft yet bright unified light source; overall brightness is medium to high. The illuminated surfaces of the subject’s face, neck and shoulders, chest area, and upper body are clear and clean. The subject’s overall brightness is noticeably higher than the background.

Refine the shaping of the face, skin, and hair. The main areas for detailed painting are the eyes, headdress, shoulder armor, gemstones, and gold-colored patterns; the hem, ribbons, and background are rendered more loosely and suggestively.

Color uses pure, luminous mineral tones such as soft ochre-red, light cinnabar, misty stone-blue, soft jade-green, light ancient gold, misty blue, rice-white, and ivory-white. The saturation is low but the brightness is moderate, creating a gorgeous and transparent effect.

The background is a medium-brightness paper or silk texture base, or a matte pigment ground; you may add a small number of totem motifs, cloud patterns, plants, or symbolic elements.
