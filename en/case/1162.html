🎨 Chinese Prompt

Nano Banana Pro 中的三个有用词：

“匹配光线”

自从它发布以来我一直在不断使用它。如果我想重新照明一个镜头并将角色融入不同的环境，我只需提供另一张图片作为参考，并要求 Nano Banana Pro 去匹配那第二个镜头的光线。

它强大的地方在于它不仅仅是重新照明。它经常会从参考图中带回细微的环境提示。例如，在有雪的参考图中，雪的痕迹可以自然而然地在场景中重新出现。

如果需要，你也可以提示它不要改变角度或构图。有时需要稍微再提示一下，但总体成功率非常高。

🎨 English Prompt

Three useful words in Nano Banana Pro:

“MATCH THE LIGHTING”

I have been using this constantly since it launched. If I want to relight a shot and integrate the character in a different environment, I simply provide another image as reference and ask Nano Banana Pro to match the lighting of that second shot.

What makes it powerful is that it does more than just relight. It often brings back subtle environmental cues from the reference. For example, in a snowy reference, traces of snow can reappear naturally in the scene.

You can always prompt it not to change angles or composition if needed. Sometimes it takes a small nudge, but overall the hit rate is very high.
