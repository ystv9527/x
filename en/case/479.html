案例479: 成为Vtuber

← Back to list

📝 Summary

成为Vtuber - Nano Banana AI图片生成案例 📥 输入：需上传一张参考图像

🎨 Chinese Prompt

使用原图创建一个虚拟的Vtuber及其直播画面。
Vtuber的发型和服装将忠实还原原图。
Vtuber的画面为2.5D画质，无需完美还原原图风格，但需要适度的立体感。
Vtuber的表情和姿势可以与原图有所差异。请让Vtuber手持游戏手柄。
仅将Vtuber的上半身放置在屏幕右下方。将正在游玩的游戏直播画面放置在屏幕中央。将聊天画面放置在屏幕左侧。
游戏画面的屏幕比例设置为较大，Vtuber的上半身显示较小。
完全忽略原图的背景和姿势。
在屏幕的上下添加虚拟的直播平台和浏览器UI。
生成图像的纵横比与原始图像的纵横比无关。

🎨 English Prompt

Create a virtual Vtuber and its live streaming scene based on the original image.
The Vtuber's hairstyle and outfit should be faithfully recreated from the original image.
The Vtuber's appearance should be in 2.5D quality, not requiring perfect style matching but with a moderate sense of depth.
The Vtuber's facial expression and pose can differ from the original image. Please have the Vtuber hold a game controller.
Place only the upper half of the Vtuber on the bottom right corner of the screen.
Position the live gameplay stream in the center of the screen.
Place the chat window on the left side of the screen.
Use a larger aspect ratio for the gameplay screen, making the Vtuber's upper body appear smaller.
Ignore the background and pose from the original image entirely.
Add virtual streaming platform UI and browser UI elements above and below the screen.
The aspect ratio of the generated image is not constrained to match the original image.
