🎨 Chinese Prompt

一组电影感的四格照片拼贴，呈现一次安静、内省的城市漫步，以吸烟为人物细节。
• 帧1：在宽阔的石阶上短暂坐着，点着烟，身后是城市灯光闪烁。
• 帧2：站在温暖的路灯下，缓慢的烟雾往上飘。
• 帧3：走过古老的欧洲建筑，烟夹在指间。
• 帧4：在关着的商店橱窗前停留，烟雾中可见倒影。
统一的穿着风格——极简黑色服装，配及踝靴。
低调灯光、柔和散景、电影感阴影、电影静态写实、编辑感氛围，1:1 纵横比。

🎨 English Prompt

A cinematic 4-panel photo collage showing a quiet, introspective city walk, with smoking as a character detail.
• Frame 1: Sitting briefly on wide stone stairs, cigarette lit, city lights glowing behind.
• Frame 2: Standing under a warm streetlamp, slow smoke drifting upward.
• Frame 3: Walking past old European buildings, cigarette held between fingers.
• Frame 4: Pausing near a closed shop window, reflection visible through smoke haze.
Consistent outfit — minimalist black clothing with ankle boots.
Low-key lighting, soft bokeh, cinematic shadows, film-still realism, editorial mood, 1:1 aspect ratio.
