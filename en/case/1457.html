🎨 Chinese Prompt

[PERSON / CHARACTER NAME].
担任一部早期2000 年代的小众经典动画系列（类似早期 Gorillaz 或 Jet Set Radio）的首席概念艺术家。

COMPOSITION (FLOATING HEAD DRAFT):

为 [CHARACTER / PERSON] 生成一个头部/胸像的单色线稿概念草图。

构图：头部严格悬浮在画布的正中央。

朝向：固定三分之四视角（3/4），以表现动感体量。

负空间：在草图周围保留大量空白（留白）。

VISUAL STYLE (ADVANCED VECTOR SKETCH):

图像必须呈现“可投入制作的概念图”风格——原始、自信，但经过打磨。

NO FILLS (Critical)：这是严格的线稿。不得使用任何颜色、渐变或灰色填充。面部、发型和服装内部必须保持白色（透明），仅由轮廓线定义。

线条质量（“描线者之手”）：
使用纯白纸上的纯黑墨线。
变化线宽：外轮廓使用粗实线条以突出角色与背景的分离。内部细节（眼睛、鼻子、发丝质感）使用细而锐利的线条。
流动感：线条应传达“快速”且富有表现力的感觉（如毛笔笔触或高级矢量笔触），而非颤抖或凌乱。属于“高级草稿”——清晰且专业。

CHARACTERIZATION (THE Y2K VIBE):

以略带棱角、前卫的面部特征进行风格化，典型于早期2000年代街头艺术。

表情：酷、冷漠、略带叛逆，或“进入状态”。

BACKGROUND：

纯白（#FFFFFF）。一张干净的纸。

🎨 English Prompt

[PERSON / CHARACTER NAME].
Act as a Lead Concept Artist for a cult classic early 2000s animated series (similar to early Gorillaz or Jet Set Radio).

COMPOSITION (FLOATING HEAD DRAFT):

Generate a Monochrome Line-Art Concept Sketch of the head/bust of [CHARACTER / PERSON].

Framing: The head floats strictly in the dead center of the canvas.

Orientation: Fixed Three-Quarter View (3/4) for dynamic volume.

Negative Space: Generous empty white space around the sketch.

VISUAL STYLE (ADVANCED VECTOR SKETCH):

The image must look like a "Production-Ready Concept Drawing" — raw, confident, but polished.

NO FILLS (Critical): This is strict LINE ART. Do not use any colors, gradients, or grey fills. The inside of the face, hair, and clothes must be white (transparent), defined only by the outlines.

Line Quality (The "Inker's Hand"):
Use Solid Black Ink on pure white paper.
Variable Line Weight: Use a THICK, bold contour line for the outer silhouette to pop the character from the background. Use THIN, razor-sharp lines for internal details (eyes, nose, hair texture).
Flow: The lines should feel "fast" and expressive (like a brush pen or sophisticated vector stroke), not shaky or messy. It is an "Advanced Draft" — distinct and professional.

CHARACTERIZATION (THE Y2K VIBE):

Stylize the character with slightly angular, edgy features typical of early 2000s street art.

Expression: Cool, detached, slightly rebellious, or "in the zone".

BACKGROUND:

Pure White (#FFFFFF). A clean sheet of paper.
